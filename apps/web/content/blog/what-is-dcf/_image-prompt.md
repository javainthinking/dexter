## Hero (1200×630, GPT Image 2 — concept-explainer pillar)

```
A modern editorial illustration for a financial analysis blog post
about discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation.

Style: dark warm aesthetic with deep blacks (#14120b) and soft
emerald accents (#4FCBA8). Minimalist, designed feel — think Stripe
blog or Linear blog. NOT corporate stock photography.

Composition: clear focal point, generous negative space, asymmetric
layout, subtle depth via overlapping geometric shapes.

Elements: a futuristic cash-flow timeline rendered as a row of
abstract bars of increasing height (representing future free cash
flows growing over time), with a curved emerald-glow arc tracing
back from the right-hand terminal bar toward time-zero on the left
(representing the discounting back to present value). The whole
composition feels like a textbook diagram crossed with magazine-cover
design — rigorous but designed.

Avoid:
- realistic human faces or hands
- literal dollar signs as icons (the shapes themselves convey value)
- brand logos
- text overlays (we add titles in HTML, not in the image)
- gradient overload, lens flares
- cliched stock-photo signals

Aspect ratio: 1200×630, suitable for OG cards and blog hero.
```
